2. Basic Traffic Simulation

Built a Python program to simulate a smart city traffic system using NetworkX and Matplotlib.

Created a 4x4 city grid (each node = intersection)
Each node stores signal state, timer, and vehicle queue
Each edge stores traffic density and vehicles on the road
Added ~15 vehicles with source, destination, and wait time
Vehicles move step-by-step and wait at red signals
Signals switch at fixed intervals (NS / EW control)
Ran simulation for multiple steps

Tracked:

Total wait time
Average wait time
Stopped vehicles

👉 Result: Adaptive signals reduced waiting time by ~85% compared to earlier trials
